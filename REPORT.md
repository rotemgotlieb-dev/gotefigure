## Questionable choices to review together (each with my recommendation)

1. **Shantell Sans as the hand voice.** It's a pro font, not *your* hand. Recommendation: keep until you photograph your hand-lettering + ~8 pen strokes (rules, underlines, arrows) — tracing those replaces the font for headlines and becomes the thing nobody can copy.
2. **Boil intensity** (165ms step, subtle wobble). Pure taste — tell me "calmer" or "livelier" and it's one number.
3. **Ink cursor trail** (0.55 alpha, 650ms fade, desktop only). Some will love it, some won't. Easy to tone down or remove.
4. **Era badges removed from cards** per "one store." The era data still exists in the model; the About page still tells the 2020 story. If you miss "back from 2020" labels, they can return as tiny ink stamps.
5. **/shop now redirects to /** (the homepage IS the shop). Old links survive; product URLs unchanged (`/shop/alien-logo-tee/`).
